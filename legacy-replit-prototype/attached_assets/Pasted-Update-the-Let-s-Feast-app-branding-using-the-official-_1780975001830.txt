APP DESIGN UPDATE:

Make the app feel modern while staying true to the Feast brand.

Use:
- White background
- Feast Red buttons
- Feast Gold accents
- Soft gray cards
- Rounded corners
- Clean shadows
- Modern sans-serif typography
- High-quality food images
- Simple clean icons

Avoid:
- Generic red colors
- Generic fork-and-knife logo
- Dated typography
- Excessive letter spacing on regular app text
- Crowded screens
- Heavy outlines everywhere

TYPOGRAPHY:

The branding guide mentions Mortend and Neusa Next Standard.

If those fonts are not available in the app, use a modern fallback:
- Headings: Montserrat, Poppins, or Inter
- Body: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif

Use the Feast-style tracking/letter spacing only for special brand moments, like:
- Splash screen
- Logo lockup
- Large Explore title
- Premium marketing cards

Do not use heavy letter spacing for:
- Buttons
- Restaurant cards
- Menus
- Cart
- Checkout
- Profile pages
